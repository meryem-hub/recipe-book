    Lemon Chicken Recipe

    Ingredients:

    - 2 chicken breasts
    - Juice of 1 fresh Lemon
    - 2 cloves garlic ,finely chopped 
    - 1 tablespoon olive olive
    - 1 teaspoon dried herbs ( like orangano )
    - salt and paper , to taste 

    Instructions :

    1.  In a small bowl, mix together the lemon juice , garlic ,olive oil ,herbs , salt and paper.
    2.  Place the chicken breasts in a dish or ziplock bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure it’s coated well.
    3.  Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or longer if you have time — it’ll taste even better!). 
    4.  ️Heat a pan over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until it’s golden and cooked through. 
    5.  ️Serve hot — it goes great with rice, salad, or roasted veggies. Enjoy!

    Tip : For extra flavor, sprinkle a little fresh parsley or a squeeze of lemon on top before serving.
